  • There are a lot of gamers who stream themselves playing games to raise funds for specific charities. Gaming can be a reliable source of non-profit fundraising as there is already a widely known stream-focused platform; Twitch.
  • There are tools in Twitch that let viewer’s contributions go directly to the charities, that helps reduce the viewer’s doubts, confusions, and even legal requirements.
  • Another streaming platform is YouTube.

Target audience: Young adults and Adults

  • Most adults have jobs or are employed, so they can donate money for the charity streams that the influencers established in order to help the situation in Sitio Bebe.
  • They’re not wasting & squandering money, rather, they’re simply assisting streamers in earning money because streaming may be considered as a job.
  • In this method, the problem of Sitio Bebe may be resolved faster with the influence of gamers.
